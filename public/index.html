about
Ha Thu is a yoga and meditation teacher based in Tokyo and Toronto. Thu is dedicated to sharing Yoga as a tool for cultivating physical and mental well being, building self-love, balance and creating community. She teaches traditional Hatha Yoga, alignment-based Vinyasa and is active in both online and in-person class.
journey to the within
As a natural seeker, in the middle of family and personal crisis, I found myself at my first yoga class, yet the postures and savasana at the end gave me a deep sense of releasing, grounding and the feeling of coming home to myself. This is when yoga became the beginning of a life long journey for me.
I am devoted to continuously­­ learning, sharing this knowledge for everyone to bring in more joy and peace into ourlives. The more I explore and teach, the more I am able to gain new wisdom from my students. I am deeply grateful to share this journey with each soul I come across in my life.
a rich practice
My teachings are grounded in the traditional lineage of Hatha Yoga married with the movement of Vinyasa Flow. I also make effort to merge the wisdom from philosophy of Buddhism into simple meditation practice that everyone can access. Making the time spending on our Yoga mat become the most precious present we give to ourselves.

I hope for each student to experience a journey that goes beyond the body and touches deeply into the heart by weaving together asana, mantra, mudra, pranayama, meditation, along with a deep connection with nature. I am passionate about the benefits for all, and believe the rituals of the physical practice of yoga and meditation within our daily routines could serve to regain our natural state of health and well-being – empowering and equipping you with the means to live a more vibrant life.
